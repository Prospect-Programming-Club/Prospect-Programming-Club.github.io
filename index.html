About


In Programming Club at Prospect High School, we learn many different computer languages and concepts. From the actual syntax code to the main ideas behind it, this club teaches the in-depth knowledge of mutliple well-known computer languages. We also participate in fun Hackathons to gain more experience and compete. Using our learning from this club, we devise and complete in porjects with code. Programming Club here at Prospect is extremely flexible and you'll have an amazing time! Whatever your past experience is with programming, this club will teach the basics and complex topics of computer languages.
